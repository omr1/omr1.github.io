      • Personal hygiene instruction

    The oral cavity is an easy place in our body that can cause problems in multiple other aspects of
    our health. Having good oral hygiene can reduce the risk of many different diseases, so it is
    highly important to practice good oral hygiene and have regular visits with the dentist. The
    sooner that good oral hygiene habits the better off the health of that patient will be. This
    poster illustrates the effects of oral hygiene on overall health.

    This poster illustrates how bad breath can be a result of many different bad habits. Some of
    these are not brushing your teeth, not brushing your tongue, not regularly seeing your dentist,
    and smoking.
