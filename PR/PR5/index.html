    Link of Interest

    The following page contains hyperlinks to Internet sites that are created and maintained by 
other organizations. These links have been included for reference and also our referring 
doctors may find them helpful. We do not We do not necessarily support the opinions 
expressed on these websites, we just listed them as a reference. We cannot guarantee that the 
information listed on these websites is accurate.

          • Research and Library Links
          • Professional Associations

    Research and Library Links

         MedicineNet  – Content-rich commercial site includes interactive groups, ask-the-doctor
         feature, medical dictionary, comprehensive drug information, medical news, disease-specific
         information, and links.

         Medscape  – This website contains resources on many different medical specialties outside of
         dentistry. Since oral health can affect many of the systems in our body, this website might be
         useful to patients trying to relate oral health to other aspects of health.

         Oncolink  - This link might be helpful to patients who have been diagnosed with oral cancer.
         The website is based on information from the University of Pennsylvania.

         Medical Matrix  – Medical Matrix is a website consisting of peer reviewed articles and scholarly
         links. Oriented toward medical professionals, but accessible to an educated lay person. The
         keyword search engine is useful to look up any topic in the medical field.


    Professional Associations

         American Academy of Oral and Maxillofacial Radiology  - This link contains all of the
         information associated with the oral and maxillofacial radiology specialty. It might be useful to
         find out any information regarding seeing a dental radiologist.

         American Association of Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery  - This link contains all of the
         information associated with the oral and maxillofacial surgery specialty. This link might be
         useful to patients who will be referred to an oral radiologist by an oral surgeon.
